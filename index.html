WHO WE ARE?

TAW-KABUI FOR A CHILD, INC. is a Non-Governmental Organization (NGO) Serving Children and Youth with Disabilities, based in the boundaries of Barangay San Jose and Tagburos, Puerto Princesa City, Palawan.
It operates programs and services both Center and Community-based, and advocates for systematic change in the life of Persons with disabilities thru rights-based approached.

Our Vision for a Better Tomorrow

VISION

Empower Families And Communities For The Promotion, Equalization Of Opportunities, And Social Inclusion Of Children And Youth With Disabilities Into The Mainstream Of Society.

MISSION

To Provide And Implement Capacity-Building Services To Families, Community Volunteers, And Stakeholders, And Promote Health, Social, And Educational Rights Of Children And Youth With Disabilities That Would Result In Participation And Full Enjoyment Of Their Rights And Privileges.

GOAL

Children And Youth With Disabilities Enjoy To The Fullest Their Human Rights And Privileges.

Community-Based Mobilization Program

Empowering Local Communities for Inclusive Development

* Training of Parents and Community Volunteers on disability and health issues.
* Training on Early detection, Prevention and Intervention of Disabilities (EDPID) to parents and community volunteers.
* Health Facilitation and referrals.

Early Intervention Program

Supporting Developmental Growth from the Start

* Provision of Learning Activities for children and youth with severe disabilities thru Home
* Management Intervention.
* Pre-Academics/Functional Academics/Functional Reading, Functional Writing, Functional Arithmetic (Numerals, Money, Measurement, Time)

Pre-vocational Training Program

Building Skills for Future Independence

* Job readiness
* Vocational Skills Training
* Internships & work-based learning
* Job placement

Our programs support children with disabilities, including Autism, ADHD, Down Syndrome, and related challenges.
